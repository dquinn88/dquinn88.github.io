Welcome to Ayrshire Cyclist

A video blog and mapping resource for you to explore the UK and Ireland's most amazing on-road and off-road cycling routes

Cycle Routes for All Distances, Terrains and Abilities

From John O' Groats to Land's End, to the NorthCoast 500,from descents in Snowdonia to climbs in the Peaks, I've spent the last 20 years enjoying the very best local and national cycle routes.

This website brings you video blogs from my trips, highlights some of the best cycling hotspots, and provides mapping resources so you can explore the exact same routes on your Google Maps, Garmin or Strava device.
